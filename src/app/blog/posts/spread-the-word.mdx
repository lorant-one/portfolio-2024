## The Design Engineers Club isn’t just growing—it’s evolving.

And that evolution starts with you.

Every movement needs momentum. Every great idea spreads because people believe in it enough to bring others in. Today, we’re making it even easier for you to do just that.

## Why invites matter
Word of mouth has always been the most powerful way to build something that lasts.

> Big corporations spend billions on marketing, ads, and algorithms that manipulate attention.

We’re doing the opposite—we grow because people _genuinely_ care.

By adding an invite component to your Portfolio or Once UI project, you’re not just sharing a link—you’re opening a door. Every invite sent brings in people who align with what we’re building, strengthening the community, the movement, and the future we’re shaping together.

## How to add an invite component to your project
We’ve made it simple. Whether you’re using Magic Portfolio, Once UI, or any Next.js project, you can drop this invite component straight into your codebase and start spreading the word instantly.

## Install Once UI (if you haven’t already)
Make sure Once UI is installed in your project:
