## Build responsive layouts easier than ever

We've made significant improvements to the <InlineCode>Flex</InlineCode> component, the cornerstone of Once UI's responsive layout system.

The most notable change is the rework of _alignItems_ and _justifyContent_ properties. These are now automatically applied based on the new _horizontal_ and _vertical_ props, ensuring consistent alignment regardless of flex-direction. We've also introduced the _center_ prop for quick horizontal and vertical centering.

To improve code semantics, we've introduced <InlineCode>Column</InlineCode> and <InlineCode>Row</InlineCode> components that extend <InlineCode>Flex</InlineCode>, making your layouts more intuitive and maintainable.
